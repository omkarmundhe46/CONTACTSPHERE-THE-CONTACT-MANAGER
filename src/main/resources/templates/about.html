Welcome to Smart Contact Manager 2.0 (SCM2.0)

Smart Contact Manager 2.0 (SCM2.0) is a modern contact management system designed to meet the growing needs of individuals and businesses. It offers an intuitive platform that allows users to store, organize, and manage their contacts effectively, providing various features to enhance user experience and ensure security.

Core Features

  • Contact Management: SCM2.0 allows users to easily add, edit, search, and delete contacts. Contacts are displayed in an organized and user-friendly format, ensuring easy access to vital information.
  • OAuth2 Authentication: SCM2.0 integrates with Google and GitHub for secure and hassle-free authentication, providing a safe and streamlined login process.
  • Dashboard Analytics: The dashboard provides a quick summary of key contact metrics, including the number of favorite contacts and total contact count, allowing users to monitor their contact database at a glance.
  • Profile Page Verification: Easily identify contacts that have verified their email and phone numbers on the profile page. This feature ensures the accuracy of contact information and builds trust between users.
  • Interactive Contact Photo: Clicking on a contact’s image reveals a detailed view where users can initiate actions like sending an email or viewing more information about the contact.
  • Favorites List: Keep your most important contacts in a favorites list for easy access and prioritize communications with them.
  • Email Notifications: Instantly send emails by clicking on a contact's photo, streamlining communication and saving time.

Advanced Functionality

  • Integration with Google and GitHub: SCM2.0 integrates OAuth2 for seamless and secure login using either Google or GitHub accounts. This guarantees strong authentication mechanisms, eliminating the need to manage passwords manually.
  • Secure Data Management: All contact data is stored securely, following industry standards for encryption and data privacy, ensuring that your personal information is protected at all times.
  • Customizable Contact Fields: SCM2.0 allows users to define custom fields, making it easier to adapt the system to specific business needs, such as adding fields for birthdays, anniversaries, or custom notes.
  • Responsive Design: Whether you’re using a desktop, tablet, or mobile device, SCM2.0 provides a responsive and seamless experience across all platforms.

Why Choose SCM2.0?

SCM2.0 is not just another contact manager. It’s a comprehensive tool built with user needs at the forefront. Whether you need a robust system to manage business contacts or simply want to keep track of personal connections, SCM2.0 offers everything you need:

  • Secure login with OAuth2 support
  • Comprehensive and customizable contact management
  • Built-in tools for prioritizing key contacts
  • Interactive and intuitive user interface
  • Fully responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop

How SCM2.0 Works

SCM2.0 allows users to manage their contacts through a clean and easy-to-navigate interface. After logging in through Google or GitHub, users are taken to their personalized dashboard. Here, they can view contact statistics and interact with their contacts by adding, editing, or deleting entries. The profile page allows for deeper insights into each contact, including verification of their contact information and the ability to send an email directly from the platform.
